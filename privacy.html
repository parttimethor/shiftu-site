Privacy

How we handle your information.

Short version: this is a marketing site. We collect only what you choose to send us, we do not sell it, and we do not run advertising trackers.

This is a plain-language summary while our formal privacy policy is being finalized. It explains how this website behaves today. For questions, email christian@shiftu.io.

What we collect

The only form on this site is the demo-request form on the Contact page. It opens your own email app with the details you typed (your name, business, email, industry, and message) addressed to us. Nothing is submitted to a server in the background. We receive that information only when you press send in your email app.

How we use it

We use what you send to reply to you, schedule a demo, and follow up about Shift Ü. We do not sell your information or share it with advertisers.

Hosting and fonts

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may log standard request data such as IP address and browser type for security and reliability. The site also loads fonts from Google Fonts, so Google may receive your IP address when those fonts load. We do not control those providers' own logs.

Cookies and tracking

We do not use advertising or analytics cookies. The site stores one small preference in your browser (your light or dark theme choice) in local storage. It never leaves your device.

Your choices

If you have emailed us and want your information updated or deleted, email christian@shiftu.io and we will take care of it.
