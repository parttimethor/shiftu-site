The operating loop

Five steps. Always running.

Nothing improvised. Each step is configured, bounded, and logged — so the work is predictable enough to actually hand over.

  1. 01

    Intake

    Capture every form, call, text, and web lead in one place — structured the moment it lands.

  2. 02

    Schedule

    Book, confirm, and remind across absolute dates and recurring cadences. No double-bookings, no no-shows.

  3. 03

    Follow up

    Chase every lead until it converts — on your cadence, with consent and opt-out built in.

  4. 04

    Bill

    Send invoices, track receivables, and nudge overdue accounts before they slip.

  5. 05

    Route

    Classify what needs a human and hand it off with the full context attached — nothing falls through.
